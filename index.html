The IEEE-CS SBC Chapter(GHRCE) Is A Student Branch Chapter Comprising Enthusiastic Members Of The IEEE Community Who Are Dedicated To Promoting Computer Science Technologies And Emerging Technology Trends That Have A Positive Social Impact On Humanity. Through The Organization Of Diverse Workshops And Events, Our Aim Is Not Only To Contribute To The Betterment Of Society, But Also To Facilitate Individual Development By Providing Opportunities To Learn New Technical Skills And Cultivate Leadership Qualities.

Volunteers, Our Mission Is To Foster Technological Advancement By Bringing People Together, While Our Vision Is To Create Abundant Opportunities For Knowledge Sharing, Technical Skill Development, Collaboration, And Networking To Contribute Towards A Better Future.
